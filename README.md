# Yum China (yum-china)

Yum China Holdings is the largest restaurant company in China by system sales, operating KFC, Pizza Hut, Taco Bell, Lavazza, Little Sheep, and Huang Ji Huang restaurants. As the exclusive licensee of Yum! Brands in mainland China, Yum China operates over 16,000 restaurants and runs Super App platforms with over 590 million loyalty members.

## APIs

Yum China does not expose public developer APIs. The KFC China Super App and Pizza Hut China digital platforms are proprietary systems managed through the company's technology organization. Digital channel integrations are conducted through internal franchise and brand partnership agreements rather than an open developer program. The entries below document these digital platforms for profiling purposes; they are not publicly callable developer APIs.

### KFC China Super App Platform

Yum China's digital ordering and loyalty platform providing members with digital ordering, personalized recommendations, loyalty points, and an AI ordering assistant. The platform has over 590 million loyalty members and processes over 55% of KFC China sales digitally.

## Digital Platform

Yum China's digital ecosystem includes:

- **Super App** — Integrated mobile platform for digital ordering and loyalty
- **Super Brain** — AI-powered operational intelligence for store managers
- **AI Ordering Assistant** — Conversational AI for KFC app users
- **Q-Smart** — Hands-free, voice-enabled AI assistant for restaurant managers (piloted at select KFC stores in 2025) covering labor scheduling, inventory management, and food safety inspection
- **590M+ Loyalty Members** — Combined KFC + Pizza Hut loyalty programs
- **55%+ Digital Sales** — Majority of sales through digital channels
